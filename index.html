Business Overview

A high-level look at who we are and what we do.

EarthWise Consultancy LTD is an agri-environmental consultancy that specializes in providing agricultural and environmental advisory services to businesses and the farming community across Ireland. Our core offerings include agronomy, environmental monitoring, agricultural analysis, grant and scheme administration, field trials, and nutrient management plans.

We leverage cutting-edge automation and custom-coded workflows to deliver faster, more cost-effective solutions. Our vision is to become Ireland’s most technologically advanced agri-environmental consultancy, offering top-quality services at competitive rates.

Agronomy

Regular on-farm visits, crop inspections, and strategic planning.

We provide regular on-farm visits, crop inspections, and long-term strategic planning for fertilizer application, yield optimization, and regulatory compliance. Our agronomy services also include field trials to test new products or methods, as well as nutrient management plans tailored to each farm’s specific soil conditions and crop requirements.

Environmental Monitoring

Soil sampling, grass measuring, and water testing.

Our monitoring services ensure that farms and businesses stay compliant with environmental regulations and promote sustainable practices. By analyzing soil, grass, and water data, we help clients adapt to changing regulations and make environmentally responsible decisions backed by solid metrics.

Grant Application Support

Streamlining paperwork for BISS, TAMS, SCIS, ACRES, and more.

We manage the administration of various grant and scheme applications, reducing the burden on our clients and minimizing errors. Our expertise in automation also makes the process faster and more cost-effective. Additionally, we partner with businesses—such as solar panel providers—to handle grant applications for their customers, offering a seamless end-to-end solution.

Consultancy for Other Ag-Advisors

Training, contract-based work, and process improvement.

We offer contract-based work, advanced automation training, and process improvement consulting specifically tailored for smaller agricultural advisory firms. By sharing our specialized agronomy knowledge and efficient workflows, we enable other advisors to enhance their service offerings and operate more effectively.
