Business Overview

A high-level look at who we are and what we do.

EarthWise Consultancy LTD is an agri-environmental consultancy that specializes in providing agricultural and environmental advisory services to businesses and the farming community across Ireland. Our core offerings include agronomy, environmental monitoring, agricultural analysis, grant and scheme administration, field trials, and nutrient management plans.

We leverage cutting-edge automation and custom-coded workflows to deliver faster, more cost-effective solutions. Our vision is to become Ireland’s most technologically advanced agri-environmental consultancy, offering top-quality services at competitive rates.

Products & Services

Delivering comprehensive solutions for agricultural and environmental needs.

We offer a range of services designed to help clients optimize their farming practices, stay compliant with regulations, and streamline their grant applications.

Agronomy

Brief Description: We provide regular on-farm visits, crop inspections, and strategic planning for fertilizer application, yield optimization, and regulatory compliance.

Further Information: Our agronomy services include field trials to test new products or methods, along with nutrient management plans tailored to each farm’s soil conditions and crop requirements.

Environmental Monitoring

Brief Description: We conduct soil sampling, grass measuring, and water testing to ensure environmental compliance and promote sustainable practices.

Further Information: By analyzing critical data, we help farms and businesses adapt to changing regulations and make environmentally responsible decisions.

Grant Application Support

Brief Description: We handle paperwork and administration for grant and scheme applications (BISS, TAMS, SCIS, ACRES, etc.), saving clients time and reducing errors.

Further Information: We also partner with businesses—such as solar panel providers—to manage their customers’ grant applications, leveraging our automation to streamline the entire process.

Consultancy for Other Ag-Advisors

Brief Description: We offer training, contract-based work, and process improvement consulting to smaller agricultural advisory firms.

Further Information: By sharing our advanced automation systems and specialized agronomy knowledge, we enable advisors to enhance their services and operate more efficiently.
