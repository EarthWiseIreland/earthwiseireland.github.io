Executive Summary

Overview, differentiators, and objectives.

Business Overview

EarthWise Consultancy LTD is an agri-environmental consultancy that specializes in providing agricultural and environmental advisory services to businesses and the farming community across Ireland. Our core offerings include agronomy, environmental monitoring, agricultural analysis, grant and scheme administration, field trials, and nutrient management plans.

Key Differentiators

  • Technology-Driven: We utilize automation and custom-coded workflows to minimize administrative tasks, enabling faster, more cost-effective service.
  • Deep Expertise: Catherine brings five years of agronomy and agri-environmental experience; Adam contributes advanced software development and process optimization skills.

Objectives & Vision

  • Short-Term: Build a client base through networking, word-of-mouth referrals, and high-quality service.
  • Medium-Term: Expand our business clientele and refine internal processes to maintain a lean but efficient operation.
  • Long-Term: Become Ireland’s most technologically advanced agri-environmental consultancy, offering top-quality services at competitive rates.

Financial Overview

  • Year 1 Revenue Goal: €50,000–€100,000
  • Funding: Self-funded by the founders; no loans or outside investment required.
  • Profitability Strategy: Operate with minimal overhead, pivot focus quickly if any service line underperforms, and keep operating costs low.

Products & Services

Core consultancy offerings for agronomy and environmental needs.

Agronomy

Regular on-farm visits, crop inspections, and long-term strategic planning for fertilizer application, yield optimization, and regulatory compliance.
Benefit: Improved margins, sustainability, and professional agronomic expertise.

Environmental Monitoring

Soil sampling, grass measuring, and water testing to ensure compliance with environmental regulations.
Benefit: Data-driven insights to maintain environmentally responsible practices and meet legal requirements.

Grant Application Support

Administration of necessary paperwork for grant and scheme applications (BISS, TAMS, SCIS, ACRES, etc.). Partnerships with businesses (e.g., solar panel providers) to manage their customers’ grant applications.
Benefit: Streamlined processes, reduced errors, and faster grant approvals.

Consultancy for Other Advisors

Training, contract-based work, and process improvement consulting for smaller agricultural advisory firms.
Benefit: Access to our advanced automation systems, specialized agronomy knowledge, and efficient workflows.

Market Analysis

Who we serve and industry trends.

Type of Customers Served

  • Individuals (Farmers): Over 130,000 active farms in Ireland seeking grant support, environmental compliance, and nutrient management.
  • Businesses: Agri-businesses, renewable energy companies, etc., needing environmental plans or compliance reporting.
  • Other Advisors: Smaller consultancies or independent advisors requiring short-term or specialized support.

Trends & Geography

  • Geography: Primarily rural Ireland, with national reach via remote work and on-site visits.
  • Key Market Trends: Increased environmental regulations, growth in renewable energy, and rising interest in data-driven farming.

Pricing Approach

Our fee structures and cost strategies.

  • Agronomy & Consultancy: Typically project-based or day-rate (e.g., €400 + VAT per day for training/contract work).
  • Soil Sampling: €10 + VAT per sample, scalable based on volume.
  • Grant Application Services: Flexible pricing based on scope and complexity, with possible volume discounts.
  • Overall Strategy: Keep overhead low to offer competitive pricing; leverage automation to reduce labor hours and pass savings on to clients.
