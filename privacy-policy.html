2. What Data We Collect

  • Personal Details: Name, email, phone number, and address.
  • Event Preferences: Selected event date/time.
  • Payment Information: Payments are processed by Stripe—we do not store or process credit card details ourselves.

3. How We Use Your Data

We collect your information to:

  • Process your event registration.
  • Send event confirmation and updates.
  • Generate attendance records or certificates.
  • Comply with legal and tax obligations (if applicable).

We do not sell or share your data with third parties for marketing purposes.

4. Payment Processing (Stripe)

Payments are securely processed via Stripe. Stripe handles all financial transactions and may collect additional data per their own privacy policy.

Stripe’s Privacy Policy

5. How Your Data is Stored

  • Registration data is stored securely in Google Sheets, accessible only to authorized personnel.
  • Form responses are processed and stored in Tally.so (GDPR-compliant servers in the EU).
  • Payment records are handled by Stripe.

6. How Long We Keep Your Data

We retain your data only as long as necessary:

  • Event registration data: Deleted 6 months after the event.
  • Financial records (for tax purposes): Retained per legal requirements.

After this period, data is permanently deleted.

7. Your GDPR Rights

As an EU resident, you have rights over your personal data, including:

  • Right to Access: Request a copy of your data.
  • Right to Rectification: Correct inaccurate or incomplete data.
  • Right to Deletion: Request removal of your data (except for legal obligations).
  • Right to Restrict Processing: Limit how your data is used.
  • Right to Data Portability: Request your data in a machine-readable format.

To exercise your rights, contact us at admin@earthwiseireland.ie.

8. Data Security Measures

We take security seriously. Steps we take include:

  • Google Sheets access is restricted to authorized staff only.
  • Tally.so and Stripe use encryption and secure processing.
  • Data is reviewed and deleted after 6 months unless legally required.

9. Cookies & Tracking

We do not use cookies on our event registration page. However, Tally.so, Stripe, or GitHub Pages may use their own cookies per their policies.
